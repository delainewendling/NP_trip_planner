How to Use this Website

EXPLORE

There are many amazing National Parks in the United States. This website is a place where you can plan a safe and fun trip to one of America's National Parks.

Click on the Explore tab and pick a park you want to explore. From there you can explore trails and campgrounds in that park

PLAN A TRIP

If you're not ready to plan a trip you can add trails to your wishlist. If you are ready to plan a trip make sure you click on 'Plan a Trip'. From there you can choose the dates and parks you want to visit.

INVITE FRIENDS

Once you have a trip, or many trips, planned you can invite friends as long as they are a user on the website.
